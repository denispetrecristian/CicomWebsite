Our story
began in 2002

When Daniela and Cornel Coman founded the ice cream factory, their biggest dream was to create a product that is synonymous with a state of well-being and could offer a feeling of happiness and delight.

Together with a team of dedicated people, they managed to create a tasty product, using only high quality ingredients.

We keep the same vision today, that of offering an ice cream created with passion. It is only that, in the meantime, we have grown continuously and became experts in what we do.
