Our Products

Whether it's waffle cones, family sized-ice cream cakes or Horeca containers, our products are meant to bring extra enjoyment and great taste to the homes of each of you

Waffle Cone Ice Cream

It is a fact: Romanians love ice cream in waffle cones. This is the reason why we decided to create the crispiest and most delicious waffle cone that will add extra tastiness to the ice cream.

We have also decided to make our own waffle cones which is why we now use two highly performant machine lines, perfectly suited for our product.

Completed by two automated ice cream production lines which give the final dessert.

Having had a look on our most recent sales from 2019 and 2020, we could say that, from the total population of Romania, one person ate at least four of our ice creams in a waffle cone in just one year.

This makes it a very popular product, doesn’t it?

Family-Sized Ice Cream Cakes

Throughout the years, there are a number of flavours we became known for.

Around 2008, we have developed a great recipe for our wild berries ice cream.

After a short time, the joy of eating melon determined us to create a cool ice cream that contains melon. It turned out that not only we liked the idea, but also those who love this fruit.

On top of it, ice cream can be really enjoyable during winter which is why we realized that walnuts could become part of the winter traditions so we’ve introduced the walnut ice cream, full of flavour and loved by many.
