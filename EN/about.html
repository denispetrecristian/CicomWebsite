About us

Founded in the early 2002, Cicom the Ice Cream Factory started from its creators dream: to deliver a product that is similar with happiness and tastiness that can only be found in the ice cream that they create. 20 years later, our company has reached the standard of an ultra modern factory with a large production capacity.

It is for this reason that we also managed to form a long-term commitment based on a trusting relationship with our partners, both nationally and internationally.

At the moment, we are proud to say that our main specialization is in the production of private label ice cream. All our activities are guided by a simple philosophy, through which we successfully delivered to all employees the pleasure of creating the ice cream.
